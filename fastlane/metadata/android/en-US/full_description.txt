I.C.E Out is an ad-free, open-source community safety tool for reporting
Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) activity after it occurs. Built with
privacy-first principles — no accounts, no tracking, no data sold.

Features:
- Anonymous report submission (no personal data collected)
- Community-sourced activity map with color-coded markers
- Report types: raid, checkpoint, surveillance, patrol, transport, other
- Know Your Rights resources & emergency contacts
- ICE facility directory
- OpenFreeMap vector tiles + Leaflet/MapLibre
- Fully offline-capable (mock mode when backend unavailable)
- Self-hostable Supabase backend — users can configure their own
- Reports expire after 72 hours
- Zero proprietary dependencies
- No analytics, no crash reporting, no third-party SDKs

All reports are view-only observations logged after incidents occur.
This app does not encourage physical interference with law enforcement operations.
