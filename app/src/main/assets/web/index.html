🛡️ Know Your Rights

You have constitutional rights regardless of immigration status. You have the right to remain silent. You do not have to open your door for ICE without a judicial warrant (signed by a judge, not just an ICE administrative warrant). You have the right to an attorney.

📱 If You See ICE Activity

Stay calm. Do not obstruct agents — it is illegal to physically interfere with an active operation. You can observe and record from a safe distance. Submit a report here after the activity has occurred to alert your community.

📋 About This App

I.C.E Out is a community safety tool for reporting ICE activity after it has occurred. It is not affiliated with any government agency. All reports are anonymous and crowd-sourced. This app does not encourage or facilitate interference with law enforcement operations.
